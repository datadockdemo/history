description The Avonmouth Hotel on the south-western side of Portview Road, Avonmouth. The building is a two-storey brick structure with a pitched tiled roof and is in an Arts and Crafts style. The building was constructed in the first decades of the twentieth century and is recorded by the Ordnance Survey third edition 1:2500-scale County Series plan (Gloucestershire Sheet LXXI.01) published in 1916. The upper floor of the building is rendered and it is currently (05/02/07) known as the Avonmouth Tavern.
