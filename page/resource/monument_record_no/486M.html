description Nos.40-42 West Street, Old Market. An attached house, or possibly a pair of houses, located on the south side of West Street. 2 storeys and attic; twin gables to front, the gables stopping short of the full frontage; 2-window range; gabled west end constructed of coursed rubble, stone stack on gable. As with the adjacent buildings, the ground-floor was set forward with a pentice roof, a central entrance and shop windows either side. Sash window to left at first-floor, 6-light window to right. 2-light window to each front gable.
